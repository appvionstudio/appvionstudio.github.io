Information We Collect

When you submit a project brief, we may collect your name, email, project type, timeline, message, browser information, and the page or contact route used.

How We Use It

We use this information to respond to inquiries, assess project fit, schedule calls, improve the website, and manage AppVion Studio's project pipeline.

Tools We Use

The website may use Firebase, FormSubmit, Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Calendly, email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, and Instagram for contact, analytics, scheduling, and communication.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by trusted services that help us receive forms, measure website performance, schedule calls, and communicate with visitors.

Retention

Project inquiries may be kept while we evaluate, discuss, deliver, or support a project. You can request deletion of your inquiry data by emailing us.

Your Choices

You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you submitted through the website.
